About

Practical analytics, not just dashboards

I work on analytics problems where the output needs to be useful for the business, not just technically correct. That usually means defining the right KPIs, structuring reliable datasets, identifying the main performance drivers, and presenting recommendations in a way stakeholders can act on quickly.

What I usually analyze

Revenue drivers, operations bottlenecks, customer behavior, funnel performance, and KPI tracking.

How I like to work

Start from the business question, define the metric logic clearly, then build clean outputs for decisions.

What employers can expect

Structured thinking, clean SQL/Python logic, and communication that stays practical and concise.
